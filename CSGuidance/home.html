Front-end

The front end is the face of an app or website, it is what the user sees when he is interacting with said app/website.Developers working on the front end will decide how the website looks and reacts to user interactions using several tools.

Back-end

"back end" of an app is what the user can't see,this means handling the business logic,working with servers and databases as well as api's to interact with each other.
