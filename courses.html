Courses

Whether you're learning on your own or planning to go to school, these are some of the core courses that any developer will want to be well versed in to be successful.

Data structures and algorithms

Data structurse and algorithms are some of the most important concepts a developer should know.Data structures are a way to store and organize data effeciently.Algorithms are an approach or method of solving problems step by step, when hiring a developer top tech companies will ensure that any potential new hire will be familiar with both of these concepts.

Programming languages

Programming languages are what developers use to actually create the program, from java to c++ to python there are many languages all all good at different things.As a developer it will be up to you to decide what language is best for your needs.

Operating Systems

Knowing your Operating system and how it works will be crucial in making sure your software runs smoothly.Being familiar with what your software will run on will save you a lot of headaches down the line.

Math

While not the most imporant skill in your arsenal, depending on your specialization.You could potentially be working with vast amounts of numbers that require a vast amount of computations.Being familiar with the math needed will aid you in your success.

Databases

For any sizeable application that handles data you will most likely be working with a database, knowing how to work with and manage a database will be important for making sure your application runs well, while also making sure the date being handled is secure.
